Diabetes:

Diabetes is a condition where blood glucose levels become high.

Common symptoms:
- Increased thirst
- Frequent urination
- Fatigue
- Slow wound healing

Prevention:
- Maintain healthy diet
- Regular exercise
- Monitor blood sugar levels

Consult doctor:
If glucose level remains high.


Heart Disease:

Heart disease affects the heart and blood vessels.

Risk factors:
- High blood pressure
- High cholesterol
- Smoking
- Diabetes
- Obesity

Symptoms:
- Chest pain
- Shortness of breath
- Irregular heartbeat

Prevention:
- Healthy lifestyle
- Control blood pressure
- Exercise regularly


Kidney Disease:

Kidney disease happens when kidneys cannot filter blood properly.

Risk factors:
- Diabetes
- High blood pressure

Symptoms:
- Swelling
- Fatigue
- Changes in urination

Prevention:
- Control sugar
- Drink enough water
- Regular checkups


Hypertension:

High blood pressure increases heart risk.

Symptoms:
- Headache
- Dizziness
- Chest discomfort

Management:
- Reduce salt
- Exercise
- Monitor BP


Asthma:

A breathing condition caused by airway inflammation.

Symptoms:
- Wheezing
- Coughing
- Shortness of breath

Prevention:
- Avoid triggers
- Follow doctor advice
