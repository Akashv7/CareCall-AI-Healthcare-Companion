Diabetes:
Diabetes happens when blood sugar levels become high.
Common symptoms include increased thirst, frequent urination,
fatigue and weight changes.

Prevention:
Maintain healthy diet, exercise regularly,
monitor glucose level.


Heart Disease:
Heart disease risk factors include high blood pressure,
high cholesterol, smoking, obesity and lack of exercise.

Symptoms:
Chest pain, breathing difficulty and irregular heartbeat.


Kidney Disease:
Kidney disease can occur due to diabetes,
high blood pressure and kidney damage.

Symptoms:
Swelling, tiredness, urine changes.


Fever:
Fever may happen due to viral infections,
bacterial infections or immune response.

Suggestions:
Drink fluids, rest properly and monitor temperature.


Headache:
Common reasons include stress, dehydration,
lack of sleep and infections.
